To measure the resistance of the PT100 element, we connect it from the CCS to ground and measure the voltage across it. The actual current of CCS should be measured using an ammeter or by measuring the voltage frop across an known resistor. The 12 bit ADC output changes by 1 LSB for change in input voltage, hence any temperature change less than 3 degrees will not be detected.
